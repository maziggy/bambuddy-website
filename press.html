Bambuddy in the Press

Independent tech press has already vetted Bambuddy as the open-source alternative to Bambu Lab's cloud.

Bambuddy is the companion app that Bambu Lab should have built from day one.

— Adam Conway, XDA-Developers

Two leading 3D-printing publications independently agreed

Within the same week of launch, both Fabbaloo (the leading independent 3D-printing news site) and 3Druck.com (the leading DACH 3D-printing publication) reached the same conclusion — without coordinating.

Fabbaloo

"The features seem to exceed those provided by Bambu Lab's own cloud. This may entice some users to simply move over to BamBuddy even without the security concerns."

3Druck.com

"The list of functions seems so extensive that it even goes beyond what Bambu Lab offers in its own cloud."
