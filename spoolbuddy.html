SpoolBuddy

An optional Raspberry Pi add-on for NFC-based spool identification and management.

What is SpoolBuddy?

SpoolBuddy is a companion hardware add-on that brings NFC-powered spool management to your Bambuddy setup. Using a Raspberry Pi and an NFC reader, SpoolBuddy lets you tag your filament spools with NFC stickers and instantly identify them by tapping — no more guessing which spool is which.

Instant Spool Identification

Tap any NFC-tagged spool to instantly pull up its details — material, color, weight, and usage history.

Raspberry Pi Powered

Runs on affordable Raspberry Pi hardware with a standard NFC reader. Easy to build and set up.

Fully Local

Just like Bambuddy itself, SpoolBuddy runs entirely on your local network. No cloud, no subscriptions.

Bambuddy Integration

Seamlessly connects to your Bambuddy instance. Spool data syncs with your inventory automatically.
