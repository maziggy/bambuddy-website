Everything You Need to Manage Your Prints

Comprehensive tools for archiving, monitoring, and automating your 3D printing workflow.

Print Archive

Automatic 3MF file archiving with metadata preservation, duplicate detection, and photo attachments for documentation.

Real-Time Monitoring

Live printer status via WebSocket, HMS error tracking, and comprehensive analytics with success rate trending.

3D Visualization

Interactive Three.js-powered model viewer lets you inspect your prints from every angle before sending to the printer.

Smart Scheduling

Drag-and-drop print queue with time-based scheduling. Integrates with Tasmota for automatic power management.

Multi-Platform Notifications

Get alerts via WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, email, Pushover, or ntfy with customizable templates and quiet hours.

Local & Private

Your data stays on your network. No cloud accounts, no external dependencies, no privacy concerns.
